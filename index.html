12 Inspirational Quotes Every Dog Lover Should Know

"1. Happiness is a warm puppy." Charles M. Schulz, cartoonist

"2. Dogs are not our whole life, but they make our lives whole." Roger A. Caras, writer and preservationist

"3. Dogs are our link to paradise. They don't know evil or jealousy or discontent. To sit with a dog on a hillside on a glorious afternoon is to be back in Eden, where doing nothing was not boring - it was peace." Milan Kundera, author

"4. A dog is the only thing on earth that loves you more than he loves himself." Josh Billings, humorist

"5. It is amazing how much love and laughter they bring into our lives and even how much closer we become with each other because of them." John Grogan, author

"6. Heaven goes by favor. If it went by merit, you would stay out and your dog would go in." Mark Twain

"7. Dogs are better than human beings because they know but do not tell." Emily Dickinson

"8. I believe in integrity. Dogs have it. Humans are sometimes lacking it." Cesar Millan, dog trainer

"9. Dogs do speak, but only to those who know how to listen." Orhan Pamuk, novelist

"10. I want to love like a dog, with unabashed devotion and complete lack of concern about what people do for a living, how much money they have, or how much they weigh." Oprah Winfrey

"11. The greatest pleasure of a dog is that you may make a fool of yourself with him, and not only will he not scold you, but he will make a fool of himself, too." Samuel Butler, author

"12. I think dogs are the most amazing creatures; they give unconditional love. For me they are the role model for being alive." Gilda Radner, comedian
